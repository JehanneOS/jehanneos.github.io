Joan of Arch

Saint Jeannes d'Arc (in Middle Franch Jehanne Darc) was burned as heretic on 30 May 1431.

Her extraordinary and dramatic story contrasts two different visions of the same faith: one that inspires and one that condemns.

Jehanne Operating System pays homage to this young peasant girl who led armies, who faced kings and inquisitors, sacrificing her life to a vision at just nineteen years of age.
